Bank AlJazira is a Saudi based financial institution that provides fully Islamic Shari’ah compliant services and solutions. Founded in 1975 with headquarters in Jeddah, Bank AlJazira offers a wide range of products and services, including Personal, Commercial, Corporate, Treasury and Private Banking. With two well established subsidiaries, AlJazira Takaful providing Islamic Insurance services and AlJazira Capital providing Brokerage & Investment Services.

Bank AlJazira is recognized as one of the leading and fast growing Shari’ah compliant financial institutions in Saudi Arabia and is well known for being customer-driven and service-oriented. In recent years, the bank has evolved tremendously, particularly in the areas of Digital Banking, Credit Cards, Real Estate Finance and Remittance services.

The Retail Banking Group continues to redefine banking services and constitutes a major proportion of the local banking market, offering innovative products and smart banking solutions through a nationwide network of 82 branches, 623 ATMs and 22,000 Merchants (point of sale).

Eng. Tarek Othman Al-Kasabi is currently the Chairman of the Board of Directors of Bank AlJazira since 2016. He obtained his bachelor’s degree in Civil Engineering in 1976 from King Saud University and has extensive experience in the financial, investment, health, industrial and construction sectors for more than 25 years.

He began his career at Kara Establishment in Makkah where he worked as Project Engineer (1976-1977) then as Project Manager (1978-1981) and as Vice President (1981-1988).

He was then appointed General Manager of Makkah Construction and Development Company and served in that capacity from 1988 to 1990. In 1990, he joined Dallah AlBaraka Company as Vice President until 2012. He is also the Chairman of the Board of Directors of Dallah Healthcare Company from 1995 until now.
