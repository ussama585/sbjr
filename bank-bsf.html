BSF is a Saudi Arabian Joint Stock Company established by Royal Decree No. M/23 dated June 1977. It has its head office in Riyadh and three regional offices in Jeddah, Al-Riyadh and Al-Khobar. Through its 84 branches, 563 ATMs and over 45,000 points of sale, BSF is committed to providing innovative and excellent services to its clients. As part of its philosophy, BSF strives to create a long term and personalized partnership with all its customers and to gaining their loyalty through recognized banking expertise, quality of service, and innovative and customized financial solutions.

Mazin AbdulRazzak
AlRomaih

Chairman

Mazin currently is the CEO and Founding Partner of Marco Strategic Advisors (Marco). Based in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, Marco is an advisory and investments firm working with leading Saudi and international organizations. His expertise lies in investment banking, capital markets and regulatory development.

Mazin serves on the Board of Directors of:

  • BSF, a Saudi commercial bank (Chairman)
  • Dr. Sulaiman AlHabib Medical Group (Vice Chairman)
  • Real Estate Development Fund (Board Member)
  • King Faisal Specialist Hospital & Research Center, (Chairman)
  • Saudi Tourism Authority (Board Member)
  • Saudi Airlines (Board Member)

Previously, Mazin was the Chairman of BSF Capital a leading financial services provider, Head of Corporate Finance at the Saudi Capital Markets Authority (CMA) leading three departments (Listing, Funds Authorization and Mergers and Acquisitions), and later he was appointed as Commissioner at the CMA. Prior to that, he was the CEO of SAMBA Capital and Investment Management Co.

Earlier in his career, Mazin worked at the Investment Banking unit of Merrill Lynch International in London, UK. Mazin started his career working at Ernst & Young LLP (Boston) in auditing and advisory services. Mazin earned his Bachelor of Science in Accounting and Financial Management from The University of Buckingham, UK
